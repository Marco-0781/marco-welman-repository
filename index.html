About

I grew up in Randfontein, finishing my schooling at Hoërskool Jan Viljoen before moving to Potchefstroom in 2023 to study BSc Information Technology at North-West University. I'm now in my final semester, with graduation this year and an Honours year in IT as the next goal.

Across my degree, three modules stood out: Networks, Artificial Intelligence, and Decision Support Systems. AI is the one I keep coming back to — less because of the coursework itself, and more because I use AI tools constantly and find there's real depth once you go past the surface. Decision Support Systems gave me a stumble along the way — I failed it in second semester — but I've gone in better prepared for the retake and I'm confident about passing it this time.
